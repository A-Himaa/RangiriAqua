About Us

RANGIRI AQUA comes under the shadow of “Asmadala” historical Rock formation, where ancient king Sithawaka Rajasinghe's horse stable was located. Also his army of soldiers rehearsed thrilling outdoor challenges in the valley of Kelani. It is a spectacular locations surrounded by panaromic greenary and waters, in the province that was always vulnerable to invasion.


Today, you will still march into the same serenity with modern approach to education through outbound training. We will use our expertise and experience mentors to satisfy the objectives of our clients.
