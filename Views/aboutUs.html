About Us

Nestled in the heart of Dambulla, Rangiri Aqua is surrounded by lush, natural greenery and embraced by the serene beauty of the scenic Ibbankatuwa Wewa. This tranquil retreat offers the perfect escape from the hustle and bustle of everyday life, allowing guests to immerse themselves in the peaceful rhythms of nature. The gentle breeze off the lake, the soothing sounds of birdsong, and the vibrant colors of the surrounding landscape create an atmosphere that is both calming and rejuvenating.
